Recall: Best Response Definition

A Best Response Correspondence (or function) is a map for player \(i\),

\[BR{i}: S_{-i}\to S_{i}\]

such that, if \(s_{i}^{BR}\in BR_{i}(s_{-i})\) (resp. \(s_{i}^{BR}= BR_{i}(s_{-i})\))

then

\[\pi_{i}(s_{i}^{BR}, s_{-i})\ge \pi_{i}(s_{i}', s_{-i})\]

for all \(s_{i}'\in S_{i}\)

Pop Quiz

  • T or F: Given some collection of opponentsâ strategies \(\hat{s}\), the payoff for any one strategy in \(BR_{i}(\hat{s})\) is the same as the payoff for any other strategy in \(BR_{i}(\hat{s})\).

Nash Equilibria â Math

To an equilibrium

  1. [Def]: A Nash Equilibrium is a set1 of mutual Best Responses

    • E.g., a strategy profile is a NE provided that all players are best responding to the other playersâ moves

  2. Mathematically,

    • \(s^{*}=(s_{1}^{*},\dots,s_{n}^{*})\) is a NE if, \(\forall i\),

      \[\pi_{i}(s_{i}^{*}, s_{-i}^{*})\ge \pi_{i}(s_{i}', s_{-i}^{*})\]

      for any \(s_{i}'\in S_{i}\)

NE (cont.)

  1. A strategy profile \(s^{*}=(s^{*}_{1},\dots,s^{*}_{n})\) is a NE provided that no player can unilaterally deviate from their strategy \(s_{i}^{*}\) an receive a strictly better payoff (holding all other playersâ moves fixed)
  • Aside: an equilibrium is always a strategy profile, not a payoff (or payoff tuple)
